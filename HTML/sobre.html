A FEW WORDS ABOUT ME

I'm Kaio Jhonatas, a dedicated telecom engineer focused on optimizing networks, ensuring seamless connectivity, and enhancing user experiences. My specialization is in advanced telecommunications solutions.

Since 2015, I've worked on various optical engineering projects, focusing on optical logic gates, magnetic field sensors with ferromagnetic materials, Sagnac interferometers for optical logic gates, and optical current sensors using carbon-coated Sagnac interferometers. My passion is advancing optical telecommunications.
